### Assignment  tasks:
---

1. Students are required to prepare a VIDEO BLOG to UTMDigital Open Day.

2. The video contributes 10% of the course assessment and it is a group assignment. The video can be created using any related software and its content is based on visit to UTMDigital Open Day.

3. Required content in the Video Blog:
a) Introduction of your group and the visit
b) Overview of the Program/Visit
c) Detail presentation on UTMDigital
d) The attended talk
e) Conclusion and Reflection
